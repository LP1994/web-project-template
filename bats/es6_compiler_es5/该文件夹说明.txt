es6_compiler_es5文件夹下都是用于将ES6转为ES5的bat命令。

如：
默认（同生产）：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

开发：
npx babel --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.dev.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

测试：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.test.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

生产：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.production.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause
