watch_compiler文件夹下都是监听ES6将其实时转换为ES5的bat命令。

如：
默认（同生产）：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -w -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

开发：
npx babel --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.dev.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -w -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

测试：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.test.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -w -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

生产：
npx babel --no-comments --compact=true --minified --config-file=../../configures/babelrc/.babelrc.production.json ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.esm.mjs -w -o ../../src/tools/js/currency_tools/CurrencyTools.compiler.js&pause

--->
OutESM.compiler.js
